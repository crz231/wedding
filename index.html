Canmore, Alberta

Nestled in the Rocky Mountains, Canmore has been a dream destination of Christine and Mark's to celebrate with the most special people in their lives. Please see below and the Travel page for suggestions around logistics.

  • Nearest Airport

    Calgary International Airport is a 1-hour drive away. Edmonton International Airport is 3.5 hours drive away. Additional transportation options are found on the Travel page.

  • Rental Cars

    Available at the airports and are recommended should there be an opportunity to explore the vicinity, including the National Parks (Banff and Jasper).

  • Rideshare

    Uber is available as a transportation option but please note that availability is limited, especially at off-peak hours and for longer routes.

  • Shuttle Bus

    Service between Calgary International Airport and Canmore is provided by the Airporter shuttle.

  • Park Pass

    To enter the town of Canmore, a park pass is not required. However, to enter Banff and Jasper National Parks, a National Park Pass must be purchased either online or at the toll station. More information here.

  • Accommodations

    Canmore and area offers many short-term rental accommodations.
